EuroBasket 2017 was held from 31 August to 17 September 2017 and was co-hosted by Finland, Israel, Romania, and Turkey.
Slovenia won EuroBasket 2017 by defeating Serbia 93–85 in the final played in Istanbul.
Serbia finished second at EuroBasket 2017 after losing to Slovenia in the final.
Spain took third place at EuroBasket 2017 by beating Russia in the bronze medal game.
Goran Dragic of Slovenia was named MVP of EuroBasket 2017.

EuroBasket 2022 took place across the Czech Republic, Georgia, Germany, and Italy.
Spain won EuroBasket 2022 after defeating France 88–76 in the final in Berlin.
France finished second at EuroBasket 2022 after losing to Spain in the final.
Germany secured third place at EuroBasket 2022 by beating Poland 82–69.
Willy Hernangómez of Spain was named MVP of EuroBasket 2022.

EuroBasket 2025 was held from 27 August to 14 September 2025 and was co-hosted by Cyprus, Finland, Latvia, and Poland.
Germany won EuroBasket 2025 by defeating Türkiye 88–83 in the final in Riga.
Türkiye finished second at EuroBasket 2025 after losing to Germany in the final.
Greece earned third place at EuroBasket 2025 by beating Finland 92–89.
Dennis Schröder of Germany was named MVP of EuroBasket 2025.
